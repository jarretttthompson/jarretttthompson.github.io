Little Fish - Animated Music/Lyric Video

This was my first (and really, only successful) attempt at creating an animation of sorts. I spent weeks in Adobe After Effects attempting to make something that was far above what my computer skills were capable of at the time.
The process involved drawing things on my iPad in Adobe Fresco and making sure that I organized the layers in a way that would allow me to tie individual elements of a character to a keyframe in After Effects.
I then watched hours of tutorials about how to use the camera within a 3d space so that I could have the lyrics sort of exist within a predefined space.
None of this was made with any kind of prior understanding about how After Effects worked at the time, so I learned a lot, and leaned heavily into mistakes if I thought they created an interesting effect.
